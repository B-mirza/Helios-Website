About Helios

"Together, we aspire to be the most sustainable campus in the entire United States"

Helios is a sustainability and renewable energy related student organization at the UW-Madison campus. As an organization, we have two goals:

  1. To work towards a more sustainable future, by making the campus more renewable, one building at a time.
  2. To get the attention of the students, and their support, in order to start an energy movement.

We plan to achieve these goals by starting exciting and ambitious renewable energy projects, by focusing on one campus building, every academic year. Together, we aspire to be the most sustainable campus in the entire United States.
